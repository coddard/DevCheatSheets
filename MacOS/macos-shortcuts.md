MacOS Keyboard Shortcuts
This document provides a comprehensive list of keyboard shortcuts for MacOS, organized into collapsible sections for easy reference. Use these shortcuts to enhance your productivity and efficiency while using your Mac. Emojis are used to make the descriptions more engaging and memorable.
Note: Click on the section titles to expand or collapse the shortcut tables.
